**Objective:** 

To analyze the profitability of the Superstore retailer to identify margin optimization opportunities and strategic recommendations.

**Business Questions**

1. **Profitability by Category/Segment:** Which product categories and customer segments are the most profitable (in terms of total profit and percentage margin on sales)?

2. **Loss Analysis:** Identify the specific products and transactions with negative profit. Are they associated with high discounts, certain regions, or customer types?

3. **Discount Effectiveness:** Is there a correlation between the discount size and profitability? At what discount percentage do sales cease to be profitable on average?

4. **Temporal and Shipping Analysis:** What is the average shipping time (shipping date - order date)? Are there any delays that impact customer satisfaction or costs? Are there seasonal patterns in sales and profitability?

5. **Geographic Analysis:** Which states, cities, or regions generate the highest profitability? Which have the highest sales volume but low profitability (possible logistical or competitive issues)?

6. **Key KPIs to Calculate:**

Profit Margin (`Profit/Sales`), Average Order Value, Sales Volume by Segment, Average Shipment Days.
